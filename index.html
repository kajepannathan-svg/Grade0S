Science of Pomodoro
  • 25 min focus — one concept only, no interruptions, phone face-down
  • 5 min break — walk, stretch, no screens (brain consolidates)
  • 4 rounds → 15-min long break
  • 4 Pomodoros = one complete 90-min study unit = ~1 topic mastered
